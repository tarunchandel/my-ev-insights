Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

Executive Summary: My EV Insights is a privacy-first application. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. All information remains exclusively on your local device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to My EV Insights ("the App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

2. No Data Collection

We built My EV Insights to be completely private. As a result:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Location Tracking: The App does not track your GPS coordinates or travel history.
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party tracking tools or cookies to monitor your behavior.
  • No Accounts: You do not need to create an account to use the App.

3. Local Storage Only

All data you input into the App—including charging sessions, meter readings, costs, and car details—is stored locally on your device using browser-based LocalStorage or IndexedDB.

  • This data never leaves your device.
  • We do not have a central database or server that stores your information.
  • If you delete the App's data from your device settings or clear your browser cache, your logged history will be permanently removed (unless you have manually exported a backup).

4. Data Portability

The App provides features to Export your data (CSV or JSON). When you use these features, the data is generated on your device and downloaded locally. We do not receive a copy of these files.

5. Third-Party Services

My EV Insights does not integrate with any third-party advertising networks or data brokers. The App is a 100% ad-free experience.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify you individually. We recommend checking the App's Settings page for links to the most current version of this policy.
